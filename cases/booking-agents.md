# Service Agents: Autonomous Booking & Ordering

## The Challenge
Hospitality and Healthcare providers in Germany were facing a skilled labor shortage, leading to missed calls and lost revenue in appointment booking and room service ordering.

## The Solution: Autonomous Service Agents
We built specialized agents for:
1.  **Lab/Hospital Booking**: Real-time calendar integration for patient appointments using natural language.
2.  **Help Desk**: First-level support for software and hardware issues, resolving 60% of tickets autonomously.
3.  **Food Ordering**: Intuitive, conversational ordering for hospitals and high-end hotels.

## The Result
- **Availability**: 24/7 service without additional headcount.
- **Booking Accuracy**: 99.8% precision in calendar scheduling.
- **Customer Satisfaction**: 4.8/5 average rating for bot interactions.

## Technical Stack
- **LLM**: Gemini 1.5 Flash (via Pydantic AI)
- **Logic**: State-machine based conversational design
- **Integration**: Stripe for payments, Google Calendar for booking
