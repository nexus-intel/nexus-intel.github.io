# Real-Time Fraud Detection Solution

> Reducing Authorised Push Payment (APP) fraud by 92% using high-performance predictive ML.

## The Challenge
A London-based fintech was seeing a surge in sophisticated social engineering attacks where customers were being tricked into authorizing fraudulent payments.

## The Solution
We implemented a multi-layered defense strategy:

- **Behavioral Biometrics**: Analysis of user interaction patterns.
- **Graph-Based Analysis**: Identifying suspicious connection patterns.
- **LLM Narrative Analysis**: Identifying common scam scripts in payment references.

### Financial Impact
- **92%** Reduction in successful fraud attempts.
- **< 0.1%** False positive rate for normal customer transactions.
- **£2.4M** Estimated annual savings in fraud reimbursements.
